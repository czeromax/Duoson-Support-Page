## Duoson — Support
### What is Duoson?
Duoson is a dual-channel audio tone generator for iPhone. It plays two independent sine wave tones simultaneously, letting you explore solfeggio frequencies, binaural-style listening, and sustained tonal meditation — all with precise control over frequency, volume, and stereo placement.

### How it works
When you open Duoson you'll see two channels — one for each tone. For each channel you can:

Set the frequency by typing a value directly or using the dial. Common solfeggio frequencies (174 Hz, 285 Hz, 396 Hz, 417 Hz, 528 Hz, 639 Hz, 741 Hz, 852 Hz, 963 Hz) are available as quick-select presets.
Adjust the volume independently per channel so you can blend the two tones to taste.
Pan the tone left or right in the stereo field — use headphones for the best experience.
Tap the play button to start both tones. The audio continues playing when your screen locks, and the lock screen media player shows playback controls so you can pause or resume without unlocking your phone.

### Tips
Use headphones. The stereo panning and dual-channel separation are most effective with in-ear or over-ear headphones.
Start low. If you're new to frequency listening, begin at a comfortable volume and give yourself a few minutes to adjust to the tone.
Session presets. Use the preset menu to quickly jump to popular frequency combinations without dialling them in manually.
Sleep timer. You can set a timer so playback stops automatically after a set duration — useful for falling asleep to tones.
Permissions
Duoson requests access to audio. It does not record, store, or transmit any audio. The audio framework the app is built on requires the permission as part of its initialisation process; no microphone input is ever used.

### Privacy
Duoson collects no personal data, creates no account, and makes no network requests. Everything runs entirely on your device.
